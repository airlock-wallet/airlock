Interaction Flow: Secure & Simple

The phone is online (untrusted). The Pi is offline (trusted).

1
Phone Initiates (BLE Wake)

Construct transactions on the Airlock App. The App transmits transaction commands to the paired Raspberry Pi wallet using Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE).

2
OLED Verify & Sign

The Pi parses the transaction offline. The SSD1306 Screen displays the amount and address. The private key signs ONLY after you press the physical button.

Critical Step
3
Return & Broadcast

Signed data is instantly returned to the App via BLE. The App broadcasts the signature to the blockchain. Your private keys never leave the Pi's memory.

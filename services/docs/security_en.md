<div class="text-subtitle1 text-weight-bold text-primary q-mb-xs">
  <i class="q-icon material-icons" style="font-size: 1em; vertical-align: middle;">wifi_off</i> 1. Complete Physical Isolation
</div>

Airlock is a cold wallet; the **core principle is never to touch the internet**.
For the safety of your assets, please strictly observe the following red lines:

- **DO NOT** insert any unknown devices unless you are certain they are safe.
- **DO NOT** connect Airlock to Wi-Fi or any other network.
- Only use a **power cable** to connect the device; avoid connecting to a computer USB port.

---

<div class="text-subtitle1 text-weight-bold text-primary q-mb-xs">
  <i class="q-icon material-icons" style="font-size: 1em; vertical-align: middle;">bluetooth</i>  2. Standardized Bluetooth Interaction
</div>

Information is exchanged between the App and the cold wallet solely via **Bluetooth**:

1. **App -> Airlock**: Sends unsigned transactions
2. **Airlock -> App**: Sends signed results
3. **App**: Broadcasts the signed transaction; transaction complete

<div class="bg-red-1 text-red-9 q-pa-sm rounded-borders q-my-sm">
  <i class="q-icon material-icons" style="font-size: 1em; vertical-align: middle;">warning</i> <b>Warning:</b>
  If the transaction information displayed on the device during the signing process does not match your actual transaction, please stop the operation immediately.
</div>

<div class="text-subtitle1 text-weight-bold text-primary q-mb-xs">
  <i class="q-icon material-icons" style="font-size: 1em; vertical-align: middle;">vpn_key</i>  3. Mnemonic Phrase Storage
</div>

The Mnemonic Phrase is your asset.

- **DO NOT Screenshot**: Do not take screenshots of the mnemonic phrase on the mobile App.
- **Physical Backup**: Please write it down on paper or use a metal mnemonic board.
- **Fire & Water Proof**: Store the backup in a safe and hidden place.

> Any request for your mnemonic phrase (including from customer service) is a scam.
